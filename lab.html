Lab & Diagnostics

Blood Tests

Complete Blood Count (CBC)

Measures red/white cells, hemoglobin, platelets

Fasting: Not required

Lipid Profile

Cholesterol, triglycerides, HDL/LDL levels

Fasting: 12 hours required

Blood Glucose

Measures sugar levels (fasting/random/HbA1c)

Fasting: 8 hours for fasting test

Liver Function Test

ALT, AST, bilirubin, proteins, enzymes

Fasting: Not required

Imaging Scans

X-Ray

Bone fractures, chest infections, dental

Duration: 5-15 minutes

Ultrasound

Abdominal organs, pregnancy, soft tissues

Duration: 15-30 minutes

CT Scan

Detailed cross-sectional body images

Duration: 10-30 minutes

MRI

Brain, spine, joints, soft tissue detail

Duration: 15-60 minutes

Specialized Tests

ECG

Heart electrical activity recording

Duration: 5-10 minutes

Endoscopy

Digestive tract examination

Duration: 15-30 minutes

Pulmonary Function

Lung capacity and function tests

Duration: 20-45 minutes

Allergy Panel

Identifies specific allergens

Duration: Blood draw + lab analysis
